About the project in depth

Our project, is about global goal 3.4, which can be split up into two major parts. Mental health, and non-communicable diseases.

We devided to focus on the mental health strand of this target, because we feel as a group that one of our biggest issues, is mental health, due to the stigma, and stereotype surronding it.
